Use this simple markdown-style format to structure your presentation content:

‚úÖ Formatting Rules

  • ## Slide X: Use this to mark the beginning of each slide
  • **Text**: Use double asterisks for titles and important text
  • - Bullet: Use hyphens for bullet points
  • Paragraphs: Regular text for descriptions and explanations
  • Blank lines: Use to separate sections for better readability
